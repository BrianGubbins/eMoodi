Diet Level:

A balanced diet has Protein/Fat/Carb values in 15/35/50 ratio
  • Poor: Alot of junk, sugary food and alcohol. Little to no vegetables or fruit. Very unbalanced ratios
  • Fair: Some notion of vegetables or fruit. Still some junk and alchohol but overall improved ratios
  • Good: Good amount of fruit and veg, junk food cut out and sensible drinking. Overall, a balanced diet

Exercise Level:

The recommended exercise guidelines are 150 minutes moderate activiy such as walking, jogging etc. or 75 minutes of vigorous such as football, running etc. or a balance of both, per week. At least two days out of the week should include strength exercises such as the ones shown in exercise suggestions.
  • Poor: No attempt to exercise whether it be through activities such as cycling or strength exercises
  • Fair: Some moderate exercise and an attempt at some strength exercises
  • Good: A mix of both moderate and vigorous activities with ample strenth training for all major muscle groups
